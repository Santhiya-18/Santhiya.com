Requirement

The client worked with complex sets of data that he wanted to present in a visual way. One of the main requirements was to track their orders and balance pending amount. The application design had to be user-friendly and modern.

Research

When I strated with online research I found many dashboard templates and Admin templates which has data visualisation and analytics. I also collaborated with the employees of IAP and understood their pain points. With the knowledge of rearch and information i got from collaboration the main aspects that i wanted to cover:

  • One stop destination for clear management
  • Best Visualization of analysis which can helps client to take better decision
  • Easy to use and track their day to day activity
  • Well organized data

Design Brainstorm

Solution

I created an application prototype that captures product, order, customers, and employee details that aims ahead to track, manage and visualise data in an efficient way which in return helps to take right organizational decisions.
