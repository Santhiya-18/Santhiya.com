PRATHIBHA

Date: 25/02/2018

Location: Stella Maris College, Chennai

Designation: President

The motto behind this project is conducting a cultural event for gifted students. We meticulously chose seven schools with physically and mentally challenged students for participating in onstage and off-stage events. To add its own touch to the event, we chose a body of volunteers of about 150 Rotaractors to train the students and enhance their talents. Around 300 school kids participated in the event.

NIRAM I

Date: 25/11/2017

Location: C.S.I. Higher Secondary School for the Deaf

Designation: President

In an effort to bring out the artistic talents of Rotaractors from non artistic backgrounds and with the vision to spread art that inspires and a mission to make the campus colourful. We launched the project NIRAM at CSI School for Deaf and Dumb , Chennai. Around 100 Rotaractors and 75 Kids potrayed their artistic skills with enthusiastic participation

NIRAM II

As a mark of grand sccess of the Project Niram we launched its sequel NIRAM II. HERS

Ongoing Project

Location: Stella Maris College, Chennai

Designation: President

Rooted against the stigma associated with menstruation, we undertook this project. I intiated this as an ongoing project in my year as President. The club collected sanitary napkins fron the students of our college, in order to distribute them to needful people. In a cycle we collected 700+ napkins and donated to around 50 girls. This also inspired my fellow Rotaract clubs and they initiated it.

Green House

Date: 11/10/2017

Location: Stella Maris College, Chennai

Designation: President

We should no longer measure our wealth and success in the graph that shows economic growth, but in the curve that shows the emissions of greenhouse gases. With an objective to create environmental awareness and to express our love for nature we launched the "Green House" Project.
