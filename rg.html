The Problem

Requirement Gathering is an important phase of any tasks. Without this it is virtually impossible to successfully meet project goals. But we are gathering requirements! why still so many distractions and confusions during the journey? Let's see it in detail.

We gather requirements in many ways by collaborating with clients, end users, research, through feedbacks sometime we get an idea which will also be a input to our work. These gatherings doesn't happen for single time we may have many call with our clients, ideas which can pour at any time. Its very important that we track these valuable points.

Lack of tracking these requirements may get us in trouble. We may forget a requirement or even if we have worked on the right requirement our client or team may not know it, at this time we may not have a strong point to speak upon. So its always good to have the requirement gatherings tracked.
